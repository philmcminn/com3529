Lectures will take the form of videos, which we will uploaded here on the first day of each week. You will be able to find these in the "Week X" pages linked to in the navigation bar. You will need to have watched the videos before each practical session of that week.

Practical sessions will take place on Fridays at 11am, and will involve real-time sessions on Blackboard Collaborate, problem sheets and video quizzes. Specific details for each week will be announced in the schedule below.

You will be able to find further details regarding the assignment on the "Assessment" page, when the assignment has been released.

You will also need to clone the GitHub repository of code examples supporting the lecture material and the practical sessions. Follow the link and the instructions to get this set up and working. (You will need it for the practical sessions.)

If you have any questions, please use the Discussion Board (linked to in the menu), which the teaching team will be monitoring regularly and replying to your queries.
